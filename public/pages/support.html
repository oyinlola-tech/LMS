Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our support team.

How do I reset my password?expand_more
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How do I access my certificates?expand_more
Certificates are available from your dashboard after completing a course with a passing grade.
Can I get a refund?expand_more
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses. Contact support to initiate a refund.
How do discussion groups work?expand_more
You can create or join topic-based groups under the Discussions page. Group messages are delivered in real-time via WebSocket. You can also subscribe to groups to get notified of new messages.
How do I follow other users?expand_more
Visit any user's profile and click the "Follow" button. You can see who you're following and who follows you from your profile page.
How do I become a tutor?expand_more
Contact us through the support page expressing your interest. Our team will review your application and credentials to onboard you as a tutor.
