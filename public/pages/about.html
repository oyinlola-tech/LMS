About LearnBridge

LearnBridge is a next-generation learning platform that bridges the gap between where you are and where you want to be. We combine expert-led instruction, AI-driven personalization, and hands-on project-based learning to create transformative educational experiences.

Founded in 2024, LearnBridge has grown to serve over 50,000 learners across 180+ countries. Our marketplace of 450+ courses spans AI, design, engineering, data science, and business — all taught by practitioners from the world's most innovative companies.

Our mission: make world-class education accessible to anyone, anywhere. We believe that learning should be personalized, practical, and career-focused.
