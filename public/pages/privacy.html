Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

LearnBridge Inc. ("LearnBridge," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our learning platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, payment information, profile details, and course progress data. We also automatically collect usage data, device information, and cookies as described in our Cookie Settings.

Encryption and Data Security

Direct Messages: All private messages between users are encrypted in transit and at rest. Message content is stored using encryption and is only decrypted for the sender and recipient. File attachments in messages are stored with encrypted filenames and restricted access URLs.

Public Discussions: Discussion threads, group messages, and replies in public forums are not encrypted. Content in these areas is visible to all participants and is stored as plain text for search and moderation purposes. Do not share sensitive personal information in public discussions.

Comments: Blog comments, course comments, and lesson comments are not encrypted. They are stored as plain text to enable moderation, search, and community features.

Content Moderation: User-generated content is automatically scanned for flagged language. Flagged content is marked internally for moderation review but is still delivered to its intended recipients. This helps maintain community safety while preserving freedom of expression.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, personalize your learning experience, process transactions, send updates and marketing communications (with your consent), and ensure platform security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform, and when required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. You can manage these preferences in your account settings or by contacting us.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Messages and content are retained to maintain conversation history. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
